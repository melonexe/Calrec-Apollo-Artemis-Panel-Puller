## About

The Panel Puller came about from my frustration with removing Artemis and Apollo panels from consoles while working in support.
This is the final version after many iterations which can be found scattered around the office in various colours (See Above!)

## How to Use

1. Loosen the panel you intend to remove's hex fasteners until the panel is free to move.
2. Choose opposed fastener pairs to lift from, to ensure the load is balanced and distributed evenly onto the tools.
3. Push the tool over the hex bolts, you will hear a nice satisfying click once they are locked on properly. (definitely didn't spend time making the click more satisfying for no reason)
4. Once secure and locked onto the bolts you can lift the panel. Ensure you have disconnected all cabling before removing fully from the console.

### Building the Tool

## As a minimum standard for strength when printing with PLA, 25% gyroid infill and 2 wall loops is required.

I personally use a brim when printing these as you can see in the 3mf file I provided.

For temperatures and speeds, that is printer dependent really, I just use the default bambu labs standard preset which works great.

Probably don't need to specify, but print with the logo face up!
